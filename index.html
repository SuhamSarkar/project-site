Day 1

  • Before we start building our machine. we started to search of some scrap. That is suitable for the frame our machine.
  • After several hours of our searching we finally found a 2 years old study table frame.
  • After cleaning the old frame, did some polishing and started to do some marking for the nuts and bolts.
  • After finishing our markings, we started the drilling process with our drilling machine in the college workshop.

Day 2

  • Finally we finished our drilling process.
  • And started looking for the scraps for the outer structure of the machine.

Day 15

  • After finishing the cutting, polishing and labeling the outer structure of the machine.
  • We screwed all the parts pf the machine all together.
  • Finally that's how the first look of the machine looks like.

Day 20

  • First look of our machine. Along with my team members.

Day 21

Day 23
